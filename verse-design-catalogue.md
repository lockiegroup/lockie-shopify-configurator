## DONATION / DENOMINATION IMAGE CODES

These are denomination logos/crests (shown on the designs sheet as a
separate group). Codes as printed:

CI, FC, FCBW, CS, URC, URC(B), F1, NAZ, PCW, CW, UFC, EPIS, BAC, MOR, DD,
M, K, F2, J, METH, G, H, ELIM, ELIM(B), CF, CF(B)

(Descriptions: CI = Church of Ireland-style cross; FC/FCBW = Free Church of
Scotland; CS = Church of Scotland burning bush; URC/URC(B) = United Reformed
Church; NAZ = Church of the Nazarene; PCW = Presbyterian Church of Wales;
CW = Presbyterian Church of Wales (Welsh); UFC = United Free Church; EPIS =
Episcopal; MOR = Moravian; METH = Methodist; ELIM = Elim Pentecostal; CF =
Congregational Federation; others are crosses/doves/Madonna variants.)
